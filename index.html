My Favourite Quotes


These are some of the most powerful words that help me go through the challenges in life

Quote 1

By Mother Teresa
  • People are often unreasonable and self-centered. Forgive them anyway.
  • If you are kind, people may accuse you of ulterior motives. Be kind anyway.
  • If you are honest, people may cheat you. Be honest anyway.
  • If you are successful, you will win some false friends and some true enemies. Succeed anyway.
  • If you are honest and frank, people may cheat you. Be honest and frank anyway.
  • What you spend years building, someone could destroy overnight. Build anyway.
  • If you find serenity and happiness, they may be jealous. Be happy anyway.
  • The good you do today, people will often forget tomorrow. Do good anyway.
  • Give the world the best you have, and it may never be enough. Give your best anyway.
  • You see, in the end, it is between you and your God. It was never between you and them anyway.

Quote 2

By Wayne Dyer

You can not expect to draw people into your life who are kind, confident and generous if you are thinking and acting in cruel, weak and selfish way.

Quote 3

By Wayne Dyer

Love is the ability and willingness to allow those who that you care to be whatever they choose for themselves without any insistence that they satisfy you.

Quote 4

By Abraham Hicks

The greatest gift you can give to anyone is your happiness.

Quote 5

By Abraham Hicks

People will love you. People will hate you. And none of it will have anything to do with you.
